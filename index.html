POCOPHONE F1

01. Product Description

From Chinese manufacturer, Xiaomi, we bring you the Pocophone F1 - a premium smartphone at a fraction of the price. Boasting an Andreno 630 GPU and the Snapdragon 845 found in most premium smartphones such as Samsung's S9, speed is definitely not an issue.


If that doesn't reel you in, its sleek design, full HD+ display and massive 4000 mAh battery should get your attention. A super fast and reliable finger-print reader, IR face unlock and 2246 x 1080 resolution make it one of 2018's best phones.

02. Hardware Specifications

The device boasts the beautiful 6.18 inch (403 pixels per inch) display OLED panel, a powerful Qualcomm Snapdragon 845 processor, 6GB RAM, 128GB internal storage in its most budget form and a massive 4000 mAh battery that supports quick charge.


It has dual sim capabilities, expandable storage, a single 20MP front facing camera, coupled with a dual 12MP & 5MP rear facing camera, bundled in Android 8's OS.
